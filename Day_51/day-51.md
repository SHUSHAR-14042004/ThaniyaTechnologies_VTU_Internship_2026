Continued theoretical studies by advancing from basic containerization to container orchestration and system architecture. Researched the fundamental differences between monolithic application designs (similar to the recent portfolio project) and decoupled microservices architectures. Studied the theoretical framework of Kubernetes (K8s), focusing on how it manages clusters of Docker containers. Reviewed core orchestration concepts, including pods, nodes, auto-scaling, and self-healing mechanisms used to maintain high availability in production environments.
