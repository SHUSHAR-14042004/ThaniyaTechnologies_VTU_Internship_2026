learning focused on understanding Express.js middleware and its role in backend applications. I studied how middleware functions act as an intermediate layer between the request and response cycle, allowing developers to process requests before they reach the main route handler. Different types of middleware such as application-level middleware, router-level middleware, and error-handling middleware were discussed. I also learned how middleware can be used for tasks like logging requests, validating user input, handling authentication, and managing errors in a structured way.
